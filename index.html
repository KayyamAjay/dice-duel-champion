Game Overview:

  • Objective: Be the first player to reach a total score of 100.

Players:

  • Two players take turns.

Gameplay:

  • Player 1 starts by rolling a six-sided die.
  • If the roll is 1, Player 1's current score becomes 0, and it's Player 2's turn.
  • If the roll is 2-6, the number is added to Player 1's current score.
  • Player 1 decides whether to roll again or hold:
    • If Player 1 holds, their current score is added to their total, and it's now Player 2's turn.
    • If Player 1 rolls again and gets a 1, their current score becomes 0, and it's Player 2's turn.

Winning:

  • The game continues until one player's total score reaches or exceeds 100.
  • The player with a total score of 100 or more wins the game.
